ABOUT

Oh, hi! If you are here to know more about Aubrey Zehui Wang, welcome! By the time you read this, Aubrey has had a Philosophy B.A. from New York University with two minors in Film Production and Psychology. Originally from Beijing, Aubrey has lived in London and Paris before moving to New York. Aubrey loves reading and discussing about aesthetics, the philosophy of Evangelion — the anime of their life, and films made by Ang Lee, Truffaut and Bresson.

In their free time, they also like listening to rock by David Bowie, Oasis, RADWIMPS and classical music. They love practicing swing dance and karate, drawing, writing film reviews, and doing random dance moves when nobody is noticing. Sometimes Aubrey still thinks they are the girl Zazie living in the film Zazie dans le Métro. Aubrey is currently working on their creative portfolio to learn more about filmmaking and teaching themself Japanese.
